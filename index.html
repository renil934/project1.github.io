Analysis of Performance of Class VIII Students in the Year 2014



How can Students Perform well in Academics?

After doing an analysis on the National Achievement Survey containing data of class VIII Students, Many interesting insights was observed which can provide a lot of transparency in knowing what can be the reasons for a student not perfoming well. Analysis was done on 185349 students across India and the main focus are on the subject scores and the overall score achieved by the students.

The main factors that were influencing the up's and down's of the students performance are:-

  • Help in household
  • Education and occupation of the students parents
  • Number of Books and subjects of a student
