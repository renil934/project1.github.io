Students Approach on Learning Social Studies Influencing the Percentage of the Subject

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Social Studies is Difficult: Students who disgrees that Social Studies is difficult has 4 percent more in Social Studies performance compared to those who agrees.
  • Historical Excursions: Students who agree in historical excursions has 3 percent more in Social Studies performance compared to those who neither agrees or disagrees disagrees.
  • Participate in Social Science: Students who agrees to participate in social science has 2.4 percent more in Social Studies performance compared to those who disagrees.
  • Small Groups in Social Studies : Students who agrees in having small groups in social science has 2 percent more in Social Studies performance compared to those who disagrees
  • Express Social Studies Views: Students who agrees in expressing Social Studies view has 2.5 percent more in Social Studies performance compared to those who disagrees


Effect of Social Studies Home Work on Students Social Studies Percentage

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Giving Social Studies HW: Giving Social Studies home work 3-4 times a week seems to have 3.2 percent more in Social Studies performance compared to giving once.
  • Correcting Social Studies HW: Correction of Social Studies home work 1-2 times a week has 3.3 percent more in Social Studies performance compared to not correcting once.


Effect of Age, Gender, Siblings and Category of Student on Social Studies Percentage Scored

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Age: Age group 13 has the maximum percentage rate and 16+ age group has the least percentage rate of Social Studies.
  • Gender: Boys and girls seems to similar in percentage rate of Social Studies
  • Category: Overall Performance increases in the order of Category 3>4>2>1>0.
  • Siblings: Student having one Sibling tend to peform more in Social Studies than the students with siblings more than three.


Influence of Parents Education and Occupation on Students performance

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Father Education: Students with father having degree or more tends to perform more and students with father who are illiterate perform the least.
  • Mother Education: Students with mother having degree or more tends to perform more and students with mother who are illiterate perform the least.
  • Father Occupation: Students with father having occupation as a Professional tends to perform more and students with father who are unemployed perform the least.
  • Mother Occupation: Students with mother occupation as a Teacher/lecturer tends to perform more and students with mother who are skilled worker perform the least
