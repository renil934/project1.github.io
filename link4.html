Students Approach on Learning Science Influencing the Percentage of the Subject



Observations and Insights

    Students who have interest in conducting and observing experiments tends to preform well.
  • Students expressing science views and solving science problems has good chance of achieving high performance rate.

Insight Observed: Students with good experimental skills and interest in gaining knowledge and ideas, so as to express it has high probability in scoring good in science.



Effect of Science Home Work and Extra Help on Students Science Percentage



Observations and Insights

  • Students with help in studies and private tuitions tends to perform well.
  • Giving science homework and correcting it 3 to 4 times a week helps students performance rate to go up.

Insight Observed: It is a good practice to provide science homework 1 to 2 or 3 to 4 times a week,so as to boost up the performance of students in science.



Effect of Age and Siblings on Student Science Percentage Scored



Observations and Insights

  • Age: Age group 13 has the maximum percentage rate and 16+ age group has the least percentage rate of Science.
  • Siblings: Student having one Sibling tend to peform more in Science than the students with siblings more than three.

Insight Observed:Students with few siblings tends to perform better which has more liklihood with overall perfomance analysis.



Effect of Usage of Computers,Library and the amount of books on Science Percentage Scored



Observations and Insights

  • Students using computers once in a week and holds 25+ books shows high probability of performing well.
  • Students using computers once in a week and uses the library once or twice a month has high performance rate.

Insight Observed:Can be observed that usage of computer once a week has a huge impact in boosting the perfomance of the students in science. More number of books held by a student has lot of chances of good results in science.
