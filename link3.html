Students Approach on Learning Language Influencing the Percentage of the Subject

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • English is Difficult: Students who disgrees that English is difficult has 8 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who agrees.
  • Read English: Students who agree in Reading English has 4 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who disagrees.
  • Dictionary to Learn: Students who use dictionary to learn has 5 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who neither agrees or disagrees.
  • Answer English WB: Students who answers English WB has 2 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who disagrees
  • Answer English Aloud: Students who answers English aloud has 3 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who disagrees


Effect of Language Home Work on Students Reading Percentage

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Giving Language HW: Giving Language home work 1-2 times a week seems to have 7 percent more in Reading performance compared to not giving once.
  • Correcting Language HW: Correction of Language home work 1-2 times a week has 6.5 percent more in Reading performance compared to not correcting once.


Effect of Age, Gender, Siblings and Category of Student on Reading Percentage Scored

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Age: Age group 13 has the maximum percentage rate and 16+ age group has the least percentage rate pf Reading.
  • Gender: Girls has 2 percent more in Reading performance compared to Boys.
  • Category: Overall Performance increases in the order of Category 4>3>1>0>2.
  • Siblings: Student having one Sibling tend to peform more in Reading than the students with siblings more than three.


Influence of Parents Education and Occupation on Students performance

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Father Education: Students with father having degree or more tends to perform more and students with father who are illiterate perform the least.
  • Mother Education: Students with mother having degree or more tends to perform more and students with mother who are illiterate perform the least.
  • Father Occupation: Students with father having occupation as a Professional tends to perform more and students with father who are unemployed perform the least.
  • Mother Occupation: Students with mother occupation as a Teacher/lecturer tends to perform more and students with mother who are farmer perform the least


Effect of Reading a Book or Magazine, Helping in the Household and Playing Games on Reading percentage Scored

From the Categorical distribution of bar plot,we can observe the following:-

  • Read a Book: Students who read books once a week has 5 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who never read books.
  • Read Magazine: Students who read magazine everyday has 7 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who never read.
  • Help in Household: Students who help in household once in a week has 5.5 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who never help in households.
  • Play Games: Students who play games has 3 percent more in Reading performance compared to those who never play games.
